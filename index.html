India Job Market Visualizer GitHub

This is a research tool that visualizes occupations from India's Periodic Labour Force Survey (PLFS), covering 123,888 workers across the Indian economy. Each rectangle's area is proportional to total employment. Color shows the selected metric — toggle between projected growth outlook, median salary, education levels, and AI exposure.

Data source: PLFS (Periodic Labour Force Survey) from MOSPI. Occupation codes based on NCO 2015. AI exposure scores are estimated based on occupation category.

Note: This is an experimental tool. Data has sampling limitations.
