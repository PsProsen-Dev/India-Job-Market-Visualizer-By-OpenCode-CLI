India Job Market Visualizer GitHub

This is a research tool that visualizes occupations from India's Periodic Labour Force Survey (PLFS), covering millions of jobs across the Indian economy. Each rectangle's area is proportional to total employment. Color shows the selected metric — toggle between projected growth outlook, median salary, education levels, and AI exposure. Click any tile to view details. This is not a report or a serious economic publication — it is a development tool for exploring Indian labour market data.

Data source: PLFS (Periodic Labour Force Survey) micro-data from MOSPI. Occupation codes are based on NCO 2015 (National Classification of Occupations). AI exposure scores are generated using LLM analysis.

Note: This is an experimental tool. Data is from PLFS survey and may have sampling limitations. AI exposure scores are rough LLM estimates, not rigorous predictions.
